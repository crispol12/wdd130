Overview

Purpose

River Fly is a company specialized in planning river tours that are used for rafting, My purpose is to provide a spectacular adventure to people who want our services, We can show you many places to practice rafting with strict security measures for your enjoyment with peace of mind..

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) my target costumers are people between 18 to 60 years, their interests should be adventures, tours and exciting travels, they should have a medium economic level. What do they want? They want to enjoy rafting and wonderful lanscapes with safety and security measures and strict protection measures. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? My audience will access my site mainly by mobile device, laptop and desktop in this order.
